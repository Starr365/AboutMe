About Me

Short Bio

I am Nzeribe, Mmesoma Stella, a Computer Science student at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka, and a passionate Front-End Web Developer proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and modern frameworks like React and Next.js. My journey into tech began from seeing how technology could simplify my father’s trading business. Today, I build meaningful digital experiences, lead with creativity, and advocate for women in STEM. Beyond tech, I am the founder of Starr Aesthetics — where I turn Pinterest ideas into framed wall art. I love building efficient, user-friendly solutions and collaborating on projects that make real impact.
